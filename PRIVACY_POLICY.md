# Privacy Policy

**TradingNewsTerminal** — operated by Bauhinia AI Limited

Last updated: March 2026

---

This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data. By using TradingNewsTerminal, you agree to the practices described below.

---

## 1. Data We Collect

We collect the following information depending on how you use the service:

- **Email address** — required for account creation and authentication.
- **Name** — optional; you may provide it for personalization.
- **Google account ID** — if you sign in using Google OAuth.
- **Wallet addresses** — if you connect a Web3 wallet.
- **Trading activity** — if you connect a brokerage account via Alpaca. Your Alpaca API keys are encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM. We never store them in plaintext.
- **Alerts and watchlists** — any alerts or watchlists you create within the platform.
- **Chat messages** — messages you send through the platform's chat features.

We do **not** collect browsing history, device fingerprints, or location data beyond what is strictly necessary for the service to function.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect for the following purposes:

- **Authentication** — to verify your identity and maintain your session.
- **Providing services** — to deliver trading news, market data, and portfolio features you have configured.
- **AI analysis** — to generate sentiment scores, summaries, and other AI-powered insights based on market data and news content.
- **Billing** — to process subscription payments through Stripe.

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

TradingNewsTerminal integrates with the following third-party services:

| Service | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Google OAuth** | Authentication (sign in with Google) |
| **Stripe** | Payment processing for Pro subscriptions |
| **Resend** | Transactional email delivery (e.g., verification emails) |
| **Alpaca Markets** | Stock trading integration — you provide your own API keys |
| **External market data providers** | Real-time and historical market data |

Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them. We share only the minimum data necessary for each integration to function.

---

## 4. Data Retention

- **News articles** — retained for 7 days, then automatically deleted.
- **User accounts** — retained until you request deletion.
- **Session data** — retained for 30 days, then automatically expired.

When you delete your account, all associated data (profile, watchlists, alerts, chat messages, encrypted API keys) is permanently removed.

---

## 5. Cookies

We use a single **HTTP-only session cookie** for authentication. This cookie is strictly necessary for the service to function.

We do **not** use:

- Tracking cookies
- Analytics cookies
- Third-party advertising cookies

---

## 6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

- **Access your data** — you can view your account information at any time through your account settings.
- **Export your data** — you can download a full export of your data (profile, alerts, trades, sessions) directly from your account settings. The export is provided in JSON format.
- **Delete your account** — you can delete your account and all associated data at any time from your account settings. Deletion is immediate and irreversible.
- **Withdraw consent** — you can disconnect third-party integrations or close your account entirely.

You may also contact us at the email address listed below to exercise any of these rights.

---

## 7. Security

We take the security of your data seriously:

- **Encryption** — all credentials (including Alpaca API keys) are encrypted using AES-256-GCM.
- **HTTPS** — all traffic is encrypted in transit. HTTPS is enforced on all endpoints.
- **Rate limiting** — API endpoints are rate-limited to prevent abuse.
- **No plaintext credential storage** — sensitive credentials are never stored in plaintext.

While we implement industry-standard security measures, no system is perfectly secure. If you discover a security vulnerability, please contact us immediately.

---

## 8. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that is likely to result in a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will:

- Notify affected users by email within **72 hours** of becoming aware of the breach.
- Describe the nature of the breach, the data involved, and the likely consequences.
- Describe the measures taken or proposed to address the breach.
- Report the breach to the relevant supervisory authority where required by applicable law.

## 9. International Data Transfers

TradingNewsTerminal is hosted on **Google Cloud Platform** in the United States (us-central1). If you access the service from outside the United States, your data will be transferred to and processed in the United States.

We rely on the following safeguards for international data transfers:

- Google Cloud's compliance with applicable data protection frameworks.
- Encryption of all data in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest (AES-256-GCM for sensitive credentials).
- Our commitment to the data protection principles described in this Privacy Policy.

By using TradingNewsTerminal, you consent to the transfer and processing of your data in the United States.
